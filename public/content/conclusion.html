Three Pillars of Modern Research

Planning First

Architectural alignment is critical. Describe intended behavior in plain language before writing code to ensure AI follows your vision.

Pilot Expertise

You are the driver. Your domain knowledge is the navigation system; AI is the engine that executes your strategic intent.

Avoid Revision Trap

Stop patching legacy debt. If a complex issue isn't resolved in two iterations, pivot. It is often faster to rebuild than to repair.

The Future is Built Today

Take-Home Message:

  • Record the journey: Process logs become your best learning assets.
  • Fail fast: If it doesn't work in 2 turns, pivot the architecture.
  • Ship fidelity: Modern users expect polished visuals, even in research.

Wait, there's one more thing...

Leihong's Secret Pro-Tips

1. Speak Your Heart 💬

Don't stress over perfect English prompts. Feel free to use Chinese (or any language you think in!) when explaining complex logic. The AI is a polyglot; it understands your intent better when you are comfortable.

2. FOMO is a Trap 🦊

There's a "groundbreaking" AI tool every 15 minutes. Follow the news, but don't feel obligated to marry every new tech. Stick to what solves your problem today. Focus on the Workflow, not just the Widget.

3. It's a Hammer, Not a Rival 🔨

AI isn't coming for your job—it's coming for your boring tasks. Keep it as your high-speed intern. You are the Architect; the AI is the incredibly fast bricklayer. Don't let the hammer tell you where to build the house!
