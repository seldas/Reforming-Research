Leihong's Secret Pro-Tips

1. Speak Your Heart

Don't stress over perfect English. Feel free to use Chinese (or your thinking language) for complex logic. The AI is a polyglot; it understands your intent better when you are comfortable.

2. FOMO is a Trap

There's a "groundbreaking" tool every 15 minutes. Follow the news, but don't marry every new tech. Stick to what solves your problem today. Focus on the Workflow, not just the Widget.

3. Hammer, Not Rival

AI isn't coming for your job—it's coming for your boring tasks. You are the Architect; the AI is the incredibly fast bricklayer. Don't let the hammer tell you where to build the house!
